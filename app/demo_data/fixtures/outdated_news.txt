HEADLINE: Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey announces new policy on platform abuse
DATELINE: San Francisco, October 2019

Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey announced today that the platform will roll out
new mechanisms to combat abuse and misinformation. The policy, which goes
into effect in November, will include stricter enforcement of existing
content rules and new appeals processes for suspended accounts.

"We believe that healthy public conversation requires both reach and
responsibility," Dorsey said in a tweet announcing the policy.

The announcement comes amid growing scrutiny of social media platforms'
handling of misinformation in the lead-up to the 2020 US election cycle.

Twitter shares closed up 2.3% on the news.
